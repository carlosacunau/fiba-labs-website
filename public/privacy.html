Last updated: March 17, 2026

FIBA Labs ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website fibalabs.com and provides AI consulting and implementation services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information you provide

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, and company name when you book a call or fill out a contact form.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent, referring URL, browser type, device type, and IP address.
  • Cookies: we use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies to understand traffic patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and schedule consultations.

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing their policies for details on their data practices.

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Contact information from inquiries is retained for up to 24 months after your last interaction.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

Our services operate from Chile. If you are accessing our website from outside Chile, your information may be transferred to and processed in Chile or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
