Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 17, 2026

FIBA Labs respects your right to control your personal data. You can request the deletion of any personal information we hold about you at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

Send an email to privacy@fibalabs.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  1. Your full name.
  2. The email address associated with your interactions with us.
  3. A description of what data you want deleted (or request full deletion).

What We Delete

Upon receiving your request, we will delete:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number).
  • Communication history (emails, messages, consultation notes).
  • Any data collected through our website or connected services.
  • WhatsApp or messaging platform interaction data.

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Exceptions

We may retain certain data if required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as:

  • Financial records required for tax or accounting compliance.
  • Data necessary to resolve ongoing disputes or enforce agreements.

If any exception applies, we will inform you of the specific data retained and the reason.
